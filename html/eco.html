We are Eco-Conscious!

Here at No 67 hairdressers we not only love your hair but our planet too. We pride ourselves in trying to be as environmentally friendly as we can and over the past few years we have removed nearly all our single use plastics and disposable waste, turning to reusable and washable alternatives.

We’ve joined forces with a great company, paper not foil, who have invented a reusable and biodegradable alternative to foil.

Where possible we try to use sustainable products, Paul Mitchell in particular are particularly good on this, which is why we stock and use within the salon and wide range of their product.

We also use Vegan colours by Montibello called Denuee. Denuee is the first and only water, ammonia-free 90% natural permanent hair dye that delivers the colour and shine of natural hair. There manifesto is one that we also believe in, a more sustainable, natural world. They are committed to driving change and minimising the impact on the environment.

At No. 67 hairdressers we also believe that being part of your community that you and your clients live is important too. Sponsoring local events and being part in local voluntary groups is another way we show we care and are truly grateful for the support we’ve been shown. Where possible we support our local Refill project shop, taking our cleaning spray bottles and washing detergent bottles to be refilled and used again. It’s another way to support a fellow local business that has the same goals as us.

In truth we love our job, clients, community and our planet. We endevour to do our best in any which way we can and hope that you, our new and loyal customers appreciate our efforts.
